Poha

Description

Poha is made up of flattened rice and it makes up a good breakfast but only if the mixture is not added.

Ingredients

  • Flattened rice
  • peanuts
  • capsicum
  • salt
  • coriander
  • potato
  • mixture if you're that sort of guy

Steps

  1. you chop all the vegetbles you want in it
  2. fry it
  3. soak the flattened rice
  4. add the flattened rice to the pan
  5. Keep stirring
